Hey there!

I'm a multimedia producer, director, editor, and photographer based in Rochester, NY. I've spent 15+ years making video content, motion graphics, and animation for global brands, working at studios like Psyop and Scholar on campaigns for Apex Legends, Clash of Clans, Aldi, Wonderful Pistachios, Bobbi Brown, Patron, Kohler, Nuveen, and others. More recently I've edited EA's FC 26 deep dive series and a T-Mobile campaign for Monks.

Outside of studio work, I produce and direct my own projects. I produced and edited "Mom, I Have Something to Tell You," a multi-award-winning documentary that screened at numerous film festivals. I've directed documentary shorts for BCG Digital Ventures and Coolburns & Co., and shot testimonial and branded content for Seth Godin's AltMBA, Zagat, and others.

I also have a degree in philosophy for some reason.
