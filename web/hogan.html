Hogan CPA Firm

Sarah Hogan, CPA · Gulf Breeze, Florida
Identity, design, copy, and build

Sarah Hogan spent eighteen years on the client side of finance before opening her own firm on the Florida panhandle. People arrive at her door carrying real anxiety about taxes and the IRS. Her practice is built on turning that fear into something a person can understand and plan around.

A CPA is chosen on trust, and the mark of that trust is a signature. It is the thing she puts on every return she stands behind. So her signature became the identity. When the site loads for the first time, the signature draws itself across a field of her brand purple, stroke by stroke in the order she actually writes it, then settles into the corner of the page and stays there as the logo.

The rest of the site keeps that promise of calm. The purple comes from her own materials rather than a palette invented for her. The headline says what she actually does for people: expert help, in your back pocket. The copy is written in her voice, plainly, about the fear people bring her and what she does with it. Nothing on the page raises the reader’s pulse, which for a tax practice is the entire job.
